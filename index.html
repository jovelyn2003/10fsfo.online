VISION

By 2040: a world-class Finance Service Field Office that is a source of pride for the Finance Center Philippine Army.

MISSION

To provide financial services and payments for all legally authorized claims against PA appropriation to organic, OPCON, and tenant units serving the 4ID, PA, and 52nd EBde, PA, in support of FCPA’s mission.

FUNCTIONS

The functions of this Office include the administrative and financial operation as follows:

Administer the payment of subsistence allowance of CAFGU Active Auxiliaries (CAAs);

Gather and collate financial data;

Provide staff financial advice to units under paying jurisdiction; and

Collect both appropriated and non-appropriated funds due to government (trust funds) and deposit same to authorized government depository bank

Beyond Finance: A Mission to Serve

The 10th Finance Service Field Office proudly took part in Agusan National High School’s Brigada Eskwela 2024, embodying the spirit of bayanihan to create a better learning environment for the next generation. Together, we build, we serve, we inspire!

With the joint effort of the 10FSFO personnel and the representatives of Agusan National High School, this collaborative initiative highlights the unwavering dedication of both institutions to service beyond duty. More than just lending a helping hand, this activity strengthens the bond between the military and the community—fostering unity, resilience, and a shared commitment to education and progress.
